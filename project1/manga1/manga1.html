New Normal

"Shortly before we were born, an infectious disease changed the world. "In the near future, hiding your mouth with a mask has become a commonplace part of daily life. Natsuki, a girl who secretly yearns for the time before the global pandemic, and her classmate Hata happen to become friends who share a small secret... The story of the two, living in a world of the "new normal" and their "new extraordinary" begins to unfold!
